Our Mission

Ecocycle is dedicated to promoting environmental sustainability through active student participation in waste management. We believe that by incentivizing recycling and proper waste disposal, we can create a cleaner, greener future while rewarding students for their positive environmental impact.

Our platform empowers students to take ownership of their ecological footprint, track their contributions, and earn tangible rewards for making environmentally conscious choices.
